Accounting

With the aid of in-house computers, our accountancy services are able to provide timely and accurate accounting information for the needs of our clients. Clients may prefer to utilise our in-house services (Ceddi Services Limited) to having their employees perform the assignment because of the following advantages:-

  • Faster provision of the financial statements;
  • Accuracy of information;
  • Better information systems and appraisal;
  • Superior presentation of financial information, thereby facilitating management decisions;
  • Confidentiality better preserved; and

The computer accounting systems we design are tailored to the individual client's needs. For clients who have their own computers, we are also able to assist in the installation of accounting systems. Where clients do not possess computerised facilities and yet wish to computerise their accounting records, they may initially utilise our computer bureau facilities, prior to the procurement of their hardware and software.

Information Technology Service

Our affiliated company (Dafinone Consulting limited) has several consultants with significant experience and expertise in installing and running computer systems and software. One important area of our specialty is the computerisation of accounting systems of companies. This is particularly helpful because most software packages require expert handling from day one to avoid unnecessary and costly mistakes. We have experts who install the Software on request, and have also developed a well-structured training scheme to ensure that our clients familiarise themselves with most features of the products within a few days of usage.

Other services rendered include advice on purchasing or assembling of hardware for our clients. This becomes necessary if the client in question does not possess a computer system, or where the present system does not meet with the requirements for the installation of the software. In most cases the system may only require a simple upgrade, as most current systems are fully compatible with the Sage range.

Company Secretarial Service

The registrar of companies is taking a very firm stand with companies that do not comply with requirements regarding the filing of accounts and statutory documents. It is therefore becoming more important that companies' public records are kept up to date. Through the secretarial services division of our firm, Dodco Services Limited (a corporate entity under the supervision of a Partner that utilises the services of a number of qualified Lawyers), Horwath Dafinone operates a complete range of company secretarial services that are necessary in order to assist our clients in fulfilling both the statutory and commercially prudent requirements of corporate affairs. Services provided include:

  • Giving advice on and assistance in formation of companies in the Federal Republic of Nigeria;
  • Providing accommodation for registered offices;
  • Acting as company secretaries and registrars;
  • Providing nominee directors and/or shareholders;
  • Managing and controlling bank accounts for clients;
  • Acting as the local representative for foreign companies.
    • The division may also liaise with the various Ministries on matters relating to:
  • the establishment of businesses in Nigeria on behalf of clients and employment of expatriates;
  • the granting of approved status to foreign capital investment;
  • the remittance of dividends and technical fees;
  • the advice and provision of information relating to new measures arising from changes in government policies.
