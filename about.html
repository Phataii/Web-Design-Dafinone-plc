Who We Are

Horwath Dafinone is one of the largest indigenous accounting and consulting firms in Nigeria. We assist public and private company clients in reaching their goals through audit, tax, advisory, risk and performance services. With over 100 personnel, we serve clients throughout Nigeria and worldwide as an independent.

member of Crowe Horwath International, one of the largest networks in the world, consisting of more than 140 independent accounting and management consulting firms with offices in more than 400 cities around the world.

CAREER

Our culture is characterized by a desire to invest in our people and to build our resources for the long-term maximization of value to our clients. We conduct regular training and testing with respect to current independence rules, as it is important to us that we maintain high ethical standards and deliver on our commitments. The commitment starts from the recruitment phase where the majority of our employees are university (or equivalent level) graduates.

We consider that training is vital in our pursuit of professional excellence, and accordingly, run in-house training courses and seminars regularly to ensure that technical competence of staff at all levels is maintained and updated. In addition, through planned "on-the-job" training, the performance and progress of individual employees is closely monitored to safeguard the quality of our services.

Bearing in mind that competent and loyal staff are valuable assets in the firm, we are very concerned with their welfare and benefits. In addition to the statutory welfare package, we also offer the following to our staff:

  • Annual Bonus and Long Service Award
  • Medical and Hospitalisation Benefits
  • Pension
  • Examination Leave
  • Education Subsidies and Training Courses
